'Power Play' challenges the myth of the almighty, untouchable Tech Bro by shrinking tech titans down to something unexpectedly ordinary: dolls. t8y created kens modeled after Mark Zuckerberg, Jeff Bezos, and Elon Musk and placed them inside a classic Barbie‑style play environment that doubles as a children’s play corner during exhibitions.

Visitors - children and adults alike - are invited to touch, arrange, restyle, or completely reinvent the roles of these miniature power figures. Through play, the installation exposes how authority is constructed through collective belief, and how easily those hierarchies can be reimagined. 'Power Play' transforms tech mythology into material for empowerment, encouraging audiences to question who holds power and how quickly it can be reshaped.
