Poisoning Reality Frame

'Poisoning Reality' is a live data poisoning experiment, which explores how easily reality can be distorted when AI systems learn from data that anyone can manipulate. We are applying different data poisoning techniques in order to alter the looks of this moth in the results of GenAI systems.

For every framed neopalpa donaldtrumpi moth we sell, we invest one more hour into data poisoning. Every frame is unique with a different moth for CHF 300, pickup in Zurich.

If you want to order a frame, please send us an email with your interest to t8ycollective (at) proton.me. We'll reply with current availability and banking details. After your payment you can pick up the frame at Bahnhof Wiedikon, Zurich, Switzerland.
