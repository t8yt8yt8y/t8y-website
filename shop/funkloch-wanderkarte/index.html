Funkloch-Wanderkarte

A hiking map to be unreachable one last time.

As part of Funklöcher, we have mapped five of Switzerland’s most beautiful dead-zone hikes and marked them with custom-made “Funkloch” (dead zone) signposts. The map is an artistic exploration and homage to places where the internet ends and the buzzing in your pocket is nothing more than an illusion.

Become part of the performance: Follow these routes into digital silence. Places without reception are a rare opportunity to observe your own digital habits and reflect on the impact of constant connectivity.

- Curated Routes: Discover handpicked trails through Switzerland’s last signal-free zones.

- Mindful Escape: A guide for anyone looking to escape digital noise and experience the quiet of nature.

- Perfect Gift: Ideal for hiking enthusiasts, digital detox seekers, and anyone eager to explore Switzerland off the beaten path.
